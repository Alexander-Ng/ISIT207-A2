Tesla Model S

Performance

  • Acceleration: The Tesla Model S Plaid can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in under 2 seconds, making it one of the fastest production cars in the world.
  • Top Speed: The top speed for the Model S Plaid is around 200 mph.
  • Horsepower: The Plaid version boasts over 1,020 horsepower.

Battery and Range

  • Battery Capacity: The Model S is equipped with a high-capacity lithium-ion battery.
  • Range: The Long Range variant can travel up to approximately 405 miles on a single charge, while the Plaid variant has a range of around 396 miles.
  • Charging: Compatible with Tesla's Supercharger network, providing up to 200 miles of range in just 15 minutes.

Interior and Features

  • Interior Design: The interior features a minimalist design with a large central touchscreen interface. High-quality materials and finishes provide a luxurious feel.
  • Seating: The Model S can comfortably seat up to five adults.
  • Infotainment: A 17-inch central touchscreen controls most of the carâ€™s functions, including navigation, media, and climate control. The car supports various entertainment options, including video streaming, gaming, and music.
  • Autopilot: Advanced driver-assistance features including Autopilot, which offers semi-autonomous driving capabilities. Full Self-Driving (FSD) package available as an upgrade, which includes features like Navigate on Autopilot, Auto Lane Change, and Summon.

Safety

  • Safety Features: Equipped with a robust set of safety features including automatic emergency braking, collision warning, blind-spot monitoring, and more. The car has achieved high safety ratings from various safety agencies.

Technology

  • Software Updates: Tesla continuously improves its vehicles with over-the-air software updates that add new features and enhance performance.
  • Tesla App: Allows remote control of various car functions, such as climate control, locking/unlocking, and location tracking.

Design

  • Exterior: Sleek and aerodynamic design with a distinctive front fascia. The Model S has a low drag coefficient, enhancing its efficiency and performance.
  • Wheels: Available with a range of wheel designs and sizes.
  • Colors: Available in various exterior colors including Pearl White Multi-Coat, Solid Black, Midnight Silver Metallic, Deep Blue Metallic, and Red Multi-Coat.
