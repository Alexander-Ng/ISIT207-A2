Chevrolet Bolt

Performance

  • Acceleration: The Chevrolet Bolt can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in approximately 6.5 seconds.
  • Top Speed: The top speed of the Chevrolet Bolt is about 93 mph.
  • Horsepower: The Bolt features a 200 horsepower electric motor.

Battery and Range

  • Battery Capacity: The Chevrolet Bolt comes with a 66 kWh lithium-ion battery.
  • Range: The Bolt offers an estimated range of up to 259 miles on a single charge.
  • Charging: Compatible with standard Level 1 and Level 2 charging, as well as DC fast charging, which can add up to 100 miles of range in about 30 minutes.

Interior and Features

  • Interior Design: The interior is designed with a focus on comfort and modernity, featuring high-quality materials and a spacious cabin.
  • Seating: The Chevrolet Bolt comfortably seats five passengers.
  • Infotainment: The car is equipped with a 10.2-inch touchscreen infotainment system that supports Apple CarPlay and Android Auto. It also includes navigation, Bluetooth, and various audio options.
  • Driver Assistance: Features like Adaptive Cruise Control, Lane Keep Assist, and Forward Collision Alert enhance driving convenience and safety.

Safety

  • Safety Features: The Chevrolet Bolt includes a suite of standard safety features such as automatic emergency braking, pedestrian detection, blind-spot monitoring, rear cross-traffic alert, and a rearview camera. It has received high safety ratings from various safety agencies.

Technology

  • Software Updates: The Chevrolet Bolt supports over-the-air updates for its infotainment system.
  • MyChevrolet Mobile App: Allows remote control of various car functions, such as climate control, charging status, and remote start.

Design

  • Exterior: The Chevrolet Bolt features a modern and aerodynamic design with a distinctive front grille and sleek lines.
  • Wheels: Available with various wheel designs and sizes.
  • Colors: Available in a range of exterior colors including Summit White, Mosaic Black Metallic, Nightfall Gray Metallic, Cajun Red Tintcoat, and Oasis Blue.
