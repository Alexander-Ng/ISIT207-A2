Nissan Leaf

Performance

  • Acceleration: The Nissan Leaf can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in approximately 7.4 seconds for the standard version and around 6.5 seconds for the Leaf Plus.
  • Top Speed: The top speed of the Nissan Leaf is about 90 mph.
  • Horsepower: The standard version has a 147 horsepower motor, while the Leaf Plus features a 214 horsepower motor.

Battery and Range

  • Battery Capacity: The Nissan Leaf comes with two battery options: a 40 kWh battery for the standard version and a 62 kWh battery for the Leaf Plus.
  • Range: The standard version offers an estimated range of up to 149 miles on a single charge, while the Leaf Plus can travel up to 226 miles.
  • Charging: Compatible with standard Level 1 and Level 2 charging, as well as DC fast charging, which can charge the battery up to 80% in about 40 minutes for the standard version and about 60 minutes for the Leaf Plus.

Interior and Features

  • Interior Design: The interior is designed with comfort and functionality in mind, featuring user-friendly controls and quality materials.
  • Seating: The Nissan Leaf comfortably seats five passengers.
  • Infotainment: The car is equipped with a 7-inch touchscreen infotainment system that supports Apple CarPlay and Android Auto. It also includes navigation, Bluetooth, and various audio options.
  • Driver Assistance: Features like ProPILOT Assist, which offers semi-autonomous driving capabilities, including adaptive cruise control and lane-keeping assistance.

Safety

  • Safety Features: The Nissan Leaf includes a suite of standard safety features such as automatic emergency braking, pedestrian detection, blind-spot monitoring, rear cross-traffic alert, and a rearview camera. It has received high safety ratings from various safety agencies.

Technology

  • Software Updates: The Nissan Leaf supports over-the-air updates for its infotainment and some vehicle features.
  • NissanConnect EV App: Allows remote control of various car functions, such as climate control, charging status, and remote start.

Design

  • Exterior: The Nissan Leaf features a modern and aerodynamic design with a distinctive V-motion grille and sleek lines.
  • Wheels: Available with various wheel designs and sizes.
  • Colors: Available in a range of exterior colors including Glacier White, Super Black, Deep Blue Pearl, Gun Metallic, and Scarlet Ember Tintcoat.
